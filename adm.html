Welcome to Little Folks School Admissions

Thank you for considering Little Folks School for your child’s early education. We welcome families who share our belief in nurturing young minds through academic excellence, creativity, values, and joyful learning.

Why Choose Little Folks School?

  • Child-centered and play-based curriculum
  • Safe and secure learning environment
  • Experienced, qualified, and caring staff
  • Focus on values, character-building, and confidence
  • Hands-on learning, critical thinking, and communication skills
  • Active participation in science, arts, sports, and field trips

Admission Process

We accept applications throughout the year, depending on seat availability. Our academic year begins in August, and admissions are offered on a rolling basis.

Eligibility

  • Playgroup (Age: 2.5 – 3.5 years)
  • Nursery (Age: 3.5 – 4.5 years)
  • Kindergarten (Age: 4.5 – 5.5 years)
  • Primary Grades 1–5 (Based on previous academic record)

Steps to Apply

  1. Visit our campus or Apply Online
  2. Fill out the admission form and submit required documents
  3. Pay the registration fee
  4. Attend an assessment & parent interview
  5. Receive confirmation and pay the admission fee

Documents Required

  • Child’s Birth Certificate (Copy)
  • 2 Recent Passport-Sized Photos
  • CNIC Copy of Parents/Guardians
  • Previous School Report Card (if applicable)
  • Vaccination Record

Important Dates – Admissions 2025–2026

  • Admissions Open: July 1, 2025
  • Assessments Begin: July 15, 2025
  • Classes Begin: August 15, 2025
