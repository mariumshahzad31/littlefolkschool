Welcome to Little Folks School

Located in Karachi, Little Folks School is committed to nurturing young minds through a balanced blend of academic excellence, character development, and creativity. Since its founding, the school has focused on providing a strong early foundation that empowers children to think independently, solve problems, and thrive in a collaborative environment.

Our School Activities

Annual Science Exhibition showcasing students' innovative projects.

Recognition ceremony for high-achieving students and families.

Organized and well-monitored exam sessions held each term.
